Our Pride

Distinguished Alumni

JNU notable Alumni placed as top government officials, corporate giants and academicians are a measure of our success. Jawaharlal Nehru University Placement Cell takes immense pride in its alumni and welcomes them to mentor, guide and and share their experience with our students.

Dr. S. Jaishankar

External Affairs Minister of India

A career diplomat, he has served as India's Foreign Secretary and ambassador to the U.S., China, and Singapore before becoming the nation's top foreign policy leader. He holds an M.Phil and Ph.D. in International Relations from JNU.

Geetanjali Shree

Author & Booker Prize Winner

Acclaimed novelist and short story writer, she became the first Indian to win the International Booker Prize for her Hindi novel 'Tomb of Sand', a milestone for Indian literature. She is an alumna of JNU's Centre for Historical Studies.

Nirmala Sitharaman

Finance Minister of India

A key figure in Indian economic policy, she has served as Defence and Finance Minister, playing a pivotal role in shaping India's fiscal and strategic direction. She completed her M.Phil in Economics from JNU.

Abhijit Banerjee

Nobel Laureate & Economist

Renowned for his work on poverty alleviation, he co-won the Nobel Prize in Economics in 2019. He is a professor at MIT known for evidence-based development research and completed his Masters in Economics at JNU.

Dr. Alok Bhattacharya

Molecular Biologist & Academic

A leading researcher in cell biology and parasitology, he has contributed extensively to biomedical science and served as a professor at JNU and member of top scientific bodies.

Admiral R. Hari Kumar

Former Chief of Naval Staff

A decorated naval officer, he headed the Indian Navy and held key leadership roles in maritime strategy, operations, and defense planning. He is an alumnus of the National Defence Academy (JNU affiliation).
