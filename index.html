Greetings from JNU Placement Cell

The JNU Placement Cell extends a warm welcome to all recruiting partners. As one of India's premier universities, Jawaharlal Nehru University is home to a diverse and accomplished student body nurtured by globally recognized faculty and a progressive academic environment.

We take pride in facilitating internship and placement opportunities between recruiters and students across disciplines. Our students bring with them not only academic excellence but also a strong sense of social responsibility, leadership, and critical thinking—qualities shaped by JNU's unique educational ethos. The Placement Cell serves as a professional bridge between top-tier talent and leading organizations, fostering partnerships built on merit, integrity, and long-term value. We look forward to collaborating with you in shaping future leaders and building professional excellence.

Placement Procedure

1

Invitation & Registration

The Placement Cell sends invitations to companies. Companies fill the Job Announcement Form (JAF).

2

Slot Allocation

Based on JAF, slots for Pre-Placement Talks (PPT) and tests are allocated.

3

Selection Process

Companies conduct interviews/tests on the allotted dates.

Final Offers

List of selected students is submitted to the Placement Cell.
